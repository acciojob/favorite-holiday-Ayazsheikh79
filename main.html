Kashmir - Paradise on Earth


Kashmir is one of the most beautiful holiday destinations in India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush green valleys. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and rich cultural heritage, making it a dream destination for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Dal Lake – Famous for its shikara rides.
  2. Gulmarg – Known for skiing.
  3. Pahalgam – Beautiful valleys.
  4. Sonamarg – Stunning mountains.
  5. Mughal Gardens – Historic gardens.

Must-Try Foods

  • Rogan Josh – Lamb curry
  • Yakhni – Yogurt curry
  • Gushtaba – Meatballs
  • Kahwa – Green tea
  • Dum Aloo – Potato dish
