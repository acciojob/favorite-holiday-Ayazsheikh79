Kashmir - Paradise on Earth


Kashmir is one of the most beautiful holiday destinations in India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush green valleys. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and rich cultural heritage, making it a dream destination for travelers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Dal Lake – Famous for its shikara rides and houseboats.
  2. Gulmarg – A popular destination for skiing and snow activities.
  3. Pahalgam – Known for its lush green valleys.
  4. Sonamarg – Offers views of glaciers and mountains.
  5. Mughal Gardens – Famous for beautiful landscapes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Rogan Josh – A flavorful lamb curry.
  • Yakhni – A yogurt-based curry.
  • Gushtaba – Meatballs in rich gravy.
  • Kahwa – Kashmiri green tea.
  • Dum Aloo – A spicy potato dish.
